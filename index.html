About Three Squirrels

三只松鼠
Sance Squirrels

Founded in 2012 in Hefei, Anhui Province, Three Squirrels (Stock Code: 300783) is a consumer-oriented, fully integrated innovation enterprise spanning primary, secondary, and tertiary industries.

Deeply focused on core online e-commerce channels while synchronously expanding offline and international markets. Three Squirrels has grown into one of China's most recognized snack brands.
