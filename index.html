About Three Squirrels

From a Small Apartment in Hefei
to 20+ Countries Worldwide

Founded in 2012 by Mr. Zhang Liaoyuan in Hefei, Anhui Province, Three Squirrels began with 5 employees and a bold vision — to build China's own world-class snack brand.

In just 2 years, we became the first nut brand on Tmall to exceed 100 million RMB in annual sales. In July 2019, Three Squirrels went public on the Shenzhen ChiNext Board (Stock Code: 300783). Today, with 5 production bases, 3,000+ employees, and 100+ SKUs, we serve distributors in over 20 countries.
