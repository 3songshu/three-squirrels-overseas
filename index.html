About Three Squirrels

三只松鼠 · Sance Squirrels

Founded in 2012 in Hefei, Anhui. A consumer-oriented, fully integrated innovation enterprise. Listed on Shenzhen Stock Exchange (Stock Code: 300783).

Three Squirrels has grown into one of China's most recognized snack brands, deeply focused on core online e-commerce channels while synchronously expanding offline and international markets.

Through continuous innovation in consumer experience and supply chain management, we deliver premium Chinese snacks to global partners with consistency and care.
