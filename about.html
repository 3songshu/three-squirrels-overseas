🌿 Our Story

From 5 Employees to a Global Brand

The journey of Three Squirrels — built on quality, driven by vision

2012 — The Beginning

Hefei, Anhui: The Spark of Three Squirrels

In April 2012, Zhang Liaoyuan (章燎源) founded Three Squirrels in a small rented apartment in Hefei, Anhui Province, with just 5 employees and a bold vision: to build China's own world-class snack brand through genuine innovation in product quality and consumer experience.

Within just 2 years, Three Squirrels achieved what many thought impossible — becoming the first nut brand on Tmall to surpass ¥100 million in annual sales — a record that shocked China's e-commerce industry.

By 2015, Three Squirrels had raised Series B funding, opened 5 regional distribution centers, and launched its first offline direct-sales stores, pioneering the O2O (Online-to-Offline) model that would become the standard for Chinese consumer brands.

  • Founded April 2012 in Hefei, Anhui Province
  • Internet-first strategy: Tmall, JD.com, self-operated app
  • First nut brand to exceed ¥100M annual Tmall sales (2014)
  • O2O model: online + offline direct-sales integration
Production Strength

5 Production Bases. 200,000+ sqm. 100+ SKUs.

Today, Three Squirrels operates 5 large-scale production bases across China with a total floor area exceeding 200,000 square meters. Production facilities are located in Hefei, Fuyang, and other strategic locations.

Our facilities feature fully automated sorting, roasting, flavoring, and packaging lines — ensuring absolute consistency from batch to batch. Every production line operates under our integrated quality management system.

With over 3,000 employees including a dedicated R&D team, we continuously develop new products that meet evolving consumer tastes in China and abroad.

  • 5 automated production bases across China
  • ISO 9001 & ISO 22000 certified facilities
  • Direct supply chain: factory straight to distributor
  • 100+ SKUs across nuts, dried fruits, snacks, candy
