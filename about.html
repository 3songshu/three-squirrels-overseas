Brand Overview

三只松鼠
Sance Squirrels

Founded in 2012 in Hefei, Anhui Province, Three Squirrels (Stock Code: 300783) is a consumer-oriented, fully integrated innovation enterprise spanning primary, secondary, and tertiary industries.

The company deeply focuses on core online e-commerce channels while synchronously expanding offline and international markets. By continuously innovating consumer experience and supply chain management, Three Squirrels has grown into one of China's most recognized snack brands.

Our Journey

From a single store in Hefei to a nationally recognized brand — a remarkable growth story.

April 2012
Founded in Hefei, Anhui
Three Squirrels opens its first store in Hefei, establishing the brand with a vision for quality Chinese snacks.
2014
Online Sales Exceed 100 Million RMB
The brand quickly gains momentum online, becoming one of the top-selling snack brands on Tmall and JD.com.
2016
Annual Sales Exceed 2 Billion RMB
Three Squirrels reaches a historic milestone, establishing itself as a leading force in China's snack industry.
2019
Stock Code 300783 — Shenzhen Stock Exchange
Officially listed on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange ChiNext Board, opening a new chapter of growth and transparency.
2022
Top 100 in China's FMCG Industry
Ranked among the top 100 fastest-moving consumer goods companies in China, recognized for innovation and scale.
2023
Officially Joins the Global Market
Three Squirrels accelerates its international expansion — exporting premium snacks to 50+ countries across 5 continents.
