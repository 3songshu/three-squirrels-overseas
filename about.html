🌿 Our Beginning

Born in Hefei,
Built for the World

In 2012, Zhang Liaoyuan started Three Squirrels in a small apartment in Hefei, Anhui Province, with just 5 employees. The vision was clear: build China's own world-class snack brand from the ground up.

Within just 2 years, Three Squirrels achieved an unprecedented milestone — becoming the first nut brand on Tmall to surpass 100 million RMB in annual sales. This wasn't luck. It was the result of obsessive focus on product quality and consumer experience.

  • Founded by Zhang Liaoyuan in Hefei, 2012
  • 5 employees with a bold vision
  • First brand to disrupt China's nut market online
🏭 Production Strength

5 Bases.
200,000+ sqm.
3,000+ People.

Today, Three Squirrels operates 5 large-scale production bases across China with a total floor area exceeding 200,000 square meters. Our workforce has grown to over 3,000 employees, including a dedicated R&D team continuously innovating new products.

Every step of the production process — from raw material sourcing to packaging — is tightly controlled under our integrated quality management system.

  • 5 automated production bases across China
  • ISO 9001 & ISO 22000 certified facilities
  • Direct supply chain, factory-to-distributor model

📅 Milestones

Our Journey

2012
The Spark — Hefei, Anhui
Zhang Liaoyuan founded Three Squirrels in a small apartment with 5 employees and a bold vision: to build China's own world-class snack brand.
2014
First Million — Tmall Record
Just 2 years after launch, Three Squirrels became the first nut brand on Tmall to exceed 100 million RMB in annual sales, setting an industry record.
2015
National Expansion
Opened 5 regional distribution centers across China. Expanded product line to 30+ SKUs including dried fruits and sea snacks, building a nationwide supply chain.
2016
Overseas Ambitions Begin
First export orders to Southeast Asia. Obtained ISO 22000 and HACCP food safety certifications, opening the door to international markets.
2019
Listed on Shenzhen Stock Exchange
July 12, 2019 — Three Squirrels went public on the Shenzhen ChiNext Board. Stock Code: 300783. Raised approximately ¥700 million in the IPO, accelerating global expansion.
2020 – Present
Global Growth
5 production bases, 200,000+ sqm, 3,000+ employees. Exporting to 20+ countries including Japan, Korea, Southeast Asia, Middle East, Europe, and North America. FDA registered, HALAL certified.
