Our Story

A Humble Beginning,
An Extraordinary Vision

Three Squirrels was founded in 2012 by Mr. Zhang Liaoyuan in Hefei, Anhui Province — starting from a small apartment with just 5 employees and a bold ambition: to build China's own world-class snack food brand.

In just 2 years, we became the first nut brand on Tmall to exceed 100 million RMB in annual sales. In 2019, Three Squirrels was listed on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange ChiNext Board (Stock Code: 300783), raising over 600 million RMB. Today, we operate 5 production bases, employ 3,000+ people, and serve distributors in over 20 countries across 5 continents.
