Three Squirrels is positioned as China's Premium Nut Expert — a trusted brand delivering high-quality Chinese snacks to the world. Our slogan, "Selected Premium Nuts, Leading Chinese Flavor", reflects our unwavering commitment to quality and our ambition to share China's finest flavors globally.

Founded in 2012 by entrepreneur Zhang Liaoyuan in Hefei, Anhui Province, Three Squirrels has grown from a single storefront into one of China's most recognized consumer brands — listed on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange (ChiNext Board, Stock Code: 300783) since 2019.

Our Journey

From One Store to Global Brand

A decade of growth, innovation, and global expansion.

April 2012
Three Squirrels Founded in Hefei
Zhang Liaoyuan opens the first Three Squirrels store in Hefei, Anhui Province. The brand's playful squirrel mascot and premium positioning quickly win over local consumers.
2015
First Production Base Established
The Hefei production base begins full-scale operation, bringing production fully in-house. Quality control and supply chain fully integrated.
2017
International Expansion Begins
First export shipments to Japan and South Korea. FDA registration and ISO 22000 certification obtained, opening doors to global markets.
2019
Listed on Shenzhen Stock Exchange
Stock Code 300783, ChiNext Board. IPO raises capital for global expansion, new production capacity, and R&D investment.
2022
Five Production Bases Nationwide
Expansion to 5 strategic bases: Hefei (HQ), Shanghai, Guangzhou, Chengdu, Shenyang. Total area exceeds 500,000 sqm, supporting annual capacity of 100,000+ metric tons.
2025
50+ Countries, 5 Continents
Operations span Asia, Middle East, Europe, North America, Africa and Oceania. Three Squirrels is now a truly global brand, delivering premium Chinese snacks to every corner of the world.
