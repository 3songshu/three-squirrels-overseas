Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about sourcing from Three Squirrels Overseas. Can't find your answer? Contact us.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)? +
We accept trial orders from as little as 100 units per SKU. For mixed-container orders (multiple products), there is no strict MOQ — contact us and we will find a workable solution. Custom packaging / private label orders typically require 500+ units per SKU.
What certifications do your products have? +
All products are certified under: HALAL, FDA (US Food and Drug Administration), ISO 22000, ISO 9001, and HACCP. Specific certificates can be provided per product and per shipment upon request.
How long does shipping take? +
Sea freight: 15–35 days from major Chinese ports (Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen) to most global destinations.
Air freight: 3–7 days (higher cost, available for urgent orders).
Express: 5–10 days to major cities worldwide.
We support FOB, CIF, DDP, and EXW incoterms.
Can I get product samples before placing an order? +
Yes. We strongly encourage sample orders so you can verify quality before committing. Sample orders typically ship within 5 business days to most countries. Sample costs and shipping fees apply. Contact us via the inquiry form to request samples.
Do you offer private label / OEM? +
Yes. We offer full private label / OEM service including: custom logo printing, bilingual labels (any language), custom packaging design, and compliance with local market regulations. MOQ for private label is typically 500+ units per SKU. Contact our export team for a custom quotation.
What are your payment terms? +
Standard payment terms for first orders: T/T 30% deposit, 70% before shipment. For established partners, we can discuss open account terms (L/C at sight, O/A). All payments are processed via bank transfer. RMB, USD, and other major currencies accepted.
Can you ship to my country / port? +
We export to 50+ countries across Asia, Middle East, Europe, North America, Africa, and Oceania. We ship to all major ports worldwide. Contact us with your destination port and we will confirm logistics feasibility and freight costs.
What packaging options are available? +
Available packaging options:
• Standard retail: Our branded packaging as-is (ready to sell)
• Custom retail: Your brand logo on our standard packaging designs
• Private label: Fully custom packaging with your brand, label language, and barcodes
• Bulk / Loose: Large-format bags or cartons without retail packaging
All packaging complies with destination country food labeling regulations.
How do I track my order? +
Once your order ships, our export team will provide: Bill of Lading (B/L), commercial invoice, packing list, and certificate of analysis. You can track your shipment via the carrier's website using the B/L number. Your dedicated export manager will provide regular updates throughout the process.
What is your response time for inquiries? +
We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days (Monday–Friday, 9 AM – 6 PM CST / UTC+8). For urgent matters, please use WhatsApp or email us directly. Sample requests are typically replied to within 2 hours.
