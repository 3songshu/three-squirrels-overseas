Our Mission

Bringing China's Finest Snacks to the World

Three Squirrels was born from a simple belief: that premium Chinese snacks deserve a place on every shelf worldwide. What started as a single Hefei storefront has grown into one of China's most recognized snack brands.

Our founder Zhang Liaoyuan built the company on three principles: quality without compromise, transparent pricing, and long-term partnership. These values guide every decision we make — from farm sourcing to final delivery.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

April 2012
Three Squirrels Founded in Hefei
Zhang Liaoyuan opens the first Three Squirrels store in Hefei, Anhui. The brand quickly gains popularity for its premium quality and distinctive squirrel mascot.
2015
First Production Base Established
The Hefei production base begins operation, bringing production fully in-house and ensuring consistent quality control.
2017
International Expansion Begins
First export shipments to Japan and South Korea. FDA and ISO 22000 certifications obtained.
2019
Listed on Shenzhen Stock Exchange
Stock Code 300783. ChiNext Board. IPO raises funds for global expansion and new production capacity.
2022
5 Production Bases Nationwide
Expansion to 5 bases: Hefei (HQ), Shanghai, Guangzhou, Chengdu, Shenyang. Total area exceeds 500,000 sqm.
2025
50+ Countries, 5 Continents
Operations span Japan, Korea, Southeast Asia, Middle East, Europe, North America and beyond. Gulfood Dubai debut.
